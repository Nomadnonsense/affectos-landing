What is AffectOS?

AffectOS is an API-first platform that simulates emotional intelligence, human decision-making, and behavioral reactions. It lets developers build products that understand, adapt to, and feel like real people.

Emotion Simulation

Model emotional reactions based on input, persona, and tone.

Decision Engine

Predict human-like choices in ethical or ambiguous scenarios.

Human-in-the-Loop

Route high-stakes or emotional queries to real vetted humans.

Use Cases

  • Empathetic chatbots for mental health & support platforms
  • Game characters with real emotional depth
  • UX research tools that understand human responses
  • Ethical simulations for AI decision-making
